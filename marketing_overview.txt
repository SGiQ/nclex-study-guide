3. DETAILED FEATURE BREAKDOWN
-----------------------------

A. AUDIO IMMERSION (The Differentiator)
   *   What it is: 16 high-quality, narrative-driven audio episodes covering the entire NCLEX test plan.
   *   Why it wins: Unlike robotic Text-to-Speech (TTS) used by competitors, our content is written for the ear. It uses storytelling, analogies, and pacing to aid retention.
   *   Key Feature: "Background Playback" allows the audio to continue playing even when the phone is locked or the user is navigating other apps.
   *   Use Case: "Sarah drives 45 minutes to nursing school. That's 1.5 hours/day of extra study time she didn't have before."

B. INTELLIGENT AI TUTOR (The value Multiplier)
   *   What it is: A fine-tuned Large Language Model (LLM) integrated directly into the dashboard and quiz interface.
   *   Context-Awareness: The AI knows exactly which question the student is looking at. They don't have to copy-paste. They just ask "Why is B wrong?" and the AI explains specifically for THAT question.
   *   Socratic Method: The AI is prompted to guide students to the answer, improving critical thinking rather than just spoon-feeding facts.
   *   Availability: 24/7 access. No waiting for email support.

C. ADAPTIVE CONTENT LIBRARY
   *   801 Practice Questions: High-yield, NCLEX-style questions (SATA, multiple choice, priority ordering).
   *   658 Spaced Repetition Flashcards: Optimized using an algorithm to show cards right before you're about to forget them.
   *   Mind Maps & Visuals: High-resolution diagrams for visual learners who struggle with text-heavy content.

D. GAMIFICATION ENGINE
   *   Streak System: Daily tracking to build habit consistency.
   *   Badge System: "Achievement Unlocked" notifications for milestones (e.g., "Speed Demon" for fast quizzes, "Night Owl" for late-night study).
